Ironbeast - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Ironbeast

Ironbeast is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle the information you provide while using the app.

Data Storage

All the information you enter into Ironbeast — including your workout logs, progress, and personal details — is stored only on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your data on any external servers or cloud services.

No Data Sharing

Ironbeast does not share your personal or workout data with third parties. Your information remains private and accessible only by you.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, please ensure your device is secure to prevent unauthorized access.

Updates

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be communicated through app updates.

If you have questions, issues, or suggestions, please open an issue on GitHub.

Thank you for trusting Ironbeast with your fitness journey!
